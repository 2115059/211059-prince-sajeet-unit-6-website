Ben Parsons

Trainee Full-Stack Developer

What does my work consist of?

As a Trainee Full-Stack Developer, I am in charge of developing and maintaining both the front-end and back-end parts of websites and applications. I collaborate closely with the development and design teams to make sure the finished product is both aesthetically pleasing and fully functioning. The skills that have helped me the most in this job is good time management. This is because this allows me to ensure everything is completed on time which not only makes my life less stressful, it pleases our clients which means they will be more likely to come back and recommend us to other businesses.

What qualifications did I need?

In order to become a Trainee Full-Stack Developer, I needed to have a solid grasp of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in addition to some expertise with server-side programming languages like PHP and Python. In most cases, a degree in computer science or a related subject is also necessary.
